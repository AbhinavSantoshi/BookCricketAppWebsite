Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2025

Welcome to Book Cricket Pro! We're committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle any information related to your use of our application.

Our Core Privacy Principles

Book Cricket Pro is designed with privacy at its core. We believe playing games shouldn't come at the cost of your personal data.

Our app:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not track detailed usage patterns
  • Does not contain any advertisements
  • Does not share data with third parties
  • Functions completely offline - no internet required
  • Stores game progress locally on your device

1. Information Collection

Book Cricket Pro is designed to function without collecting personal information. All game data, including your scores, statistics, and preferences, are stored locally on your device.

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not ask for or collect:

  • Names or contact information
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Social media profiles
  • Any personal identifiers

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The app may collect basic, anonymized usage statistics such as:

  • App crashes and errors for debugging purposes
  • Basic device information needed for app functionality (device type, operating system version)
  • Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to help us improve the app

2. Use of Information

The limited information we collect is used only to:

  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on anonymous usage trends

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The limited anonymous data we collect is used solely for app improvement and is not shared with:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Other third-party services

Exceptions may occur only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights
  • To prevent fraud or safety issues

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect against unauthorized access to app data. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Links

Book Cricket Pro does not contain links to other websites or services. All gameplay happens within the app environment.

6. Children's Privacy

Book Cricket Pro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. As we do not collect personal information from any users, including children, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. App Permissions

Book Cricket Pro requests minimal permissions required for basic functionality:

  • Storage: To save game data locally on your device

We do not request permissions for:

  • Location
  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts
  • Phone state

9. Your Choices

You have control over your data:

  • All game data is stored locally and can be deleted by uninstalling the app
