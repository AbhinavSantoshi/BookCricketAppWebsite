Frequently Asked Questions

Is Play Book Cricket available for iPhone?

Currently, Play Book Cricket is available exclusively for Android devices. We are considering an iOS version for the future based on user interest.

How do I play book cricket on the app?

Simply download the app, select your teams, and start flipping! The app simulates the classic book cricket experience where flipping pages reveals your runs scored. You'll find comprehensive instructions within the app.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No, once downloaded, Play Book Cricket works completely offline. You can enjoy the game anywhere, anytime without needing an internet connection.

Is the app free to download?

Play Book Cricket is free to download with basic features. Premium features are available through a small one-time purchase.
