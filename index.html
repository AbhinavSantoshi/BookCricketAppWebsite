Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play Book Cricket?

Book Cricket is played by flipping pages of a book and using the last digit of the page number as runs scored. In our app, we've recreated this experience with digital page flipping. Even numbers (2,4,6,8) score runs, while odd numbers (1,3,5,7,9) count as dot balls. A page ending with '0' means you're out!

Is the app available on both iOS and Android?

Yes! Book Cricket Pro is available on both the Apple App Store for iOS devices and Google Play Store for Android devices. The game experience is identical across both platforms.

Do I need an internet connection to play?

Not at all! Book Cricket Pro works completely offline. Once you've downloaded the app, you can play anytime, anywhere without needing an internet connection. This makes it perfect for travel, subway commutes, or anywhere with spotty service.

Does the app have ads?

Book Cricket Pro is completely ad-free! We believe in providing a clean, uninterrupted gaming experience without annoying pop-ups or advertisements. This is one of our core features that players love about our app.

Will the app drain my battery quickly?

No, we've optimized Book Cricket Pro to be extremely battery-friendly. The app is designed to consume minimal resources so you can enjoy extended gaming sessions without worrying about your battery life.
