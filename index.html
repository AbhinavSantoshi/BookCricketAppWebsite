Frequently Asked Questions

How do you play Book Cricket?

Book Cricket is played by flipping pages of a book and using the last digit of the page number as runs scored. In our app, we've recreated this experience with digital page flipping. Even numbers (2,4,6,8) score runs, while odd numbers (1,3,5,7,9) count as dot balls. A page ending with '0' means you're out!

Is the app available on both iOS and Android?

Yes! Book Cricket Pro is available on both the Apple App Store for iOS devices and Google Play Store for Android devices. The game experience is identical across both platforms.

Do I need an internet connection to play?

Not at all! Book Cricket Pro works completely offline. Once you've downloaded the app, you can play anytime, anywhere without needing an internet connection.

Can I create my own custom teams?

Absolutely! The app allows you to create custom teams with player names of your choice. You can also customize player attributes in the premium version of the app.

Is there a multiplayer mode?

Yes, you can play with friends on the same device by taking turns. We're also working on an online multiplayer feature that will be available in a future update!

Information We Don't Collect

We're happy to inform you that Book Cricket is designed with your privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location
  • We do not require account creation to use the app
  • We do not track your usage habits or playing patterns

The only information our app may collect includes:

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your information on external servers.

Book Cricket does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising services.
